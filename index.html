Withdrawal Strategy
When you retire, funds are withdrawn in this tax-optimized order (within each account):

1. STRC/SATA dividends (lower-return preferred first)
2. Cash
3. Stocks
4. STRC/SATA principal (lower-return preferred first)
5. FBTC (Bitcoin ETF)
6. MSTR (leveraged Bitcoin)
7. Bitcoin (preserved longest)

This maximizes Bitcoin growth while using lower-growth assets first. Before age 59.5, only taxable/self-custody accounts are used to avoid penalties.
